Biography

I am a quantitative ecologist working in the mathematical and statistical analysis of biological data from real world applications. In particular, my goal is to develop methods for robustly teasing apart the effects of ecological interactions from abiotic effects and sampling variance in population and community time series. Since I work with real world (messy) data, I have a genuine interest in statistics and I am always keen to learn new techniques and methods to cope with this uncertainty
